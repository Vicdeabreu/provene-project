About Us

Protecting you

Biosafety Security LatAm is a Panamanian company, inspired by the multitude of challenges and threats faced by its founders’ assignments abroad in high conflict zones around the world in the last 25 years. From inception, Biosafety Security LatAm has applied the training, skills and knowledge of the company founders to deepen and grow our capabilities against the growing vulnerabilities businesses, institutions and individuals are facing. We provide reliable, robust and the latest cutting-edge technology in security, cyber-security, Biosafety, and security intelligence. As part of our vision to protect life, reduce risks and practice security, we seek to expand our capable services to more countries in Latin America. Our specialized technology along with our know-how of innovative security solutions can support your success and give you sought-after security assurances that meet the demands of various situations.

Some of our services include

  • Fly safe: security for aviation, critical & strategic sites
  • Smart borders: border patrol & border control
  • Cyber security: for institutional or corporate networks (for databases and command center infrastructures).
  • Smart Integration: enabling Intelligence Systems for early warning, detection, and prevention
  • Due to the Covid-19 pandemic and the need for strengthening against biological threats, our expertise covers an additional Biosafety layer for our customers.

Working with the best

Many of our board advisors are formerly “high ranked key figures” from security and intelligence agencies with years of experience in security projects all over the world. As skilled professionals with specialized research and development expertise, they have remained at the forefront of security awareness and this has enabled our company with advantages over the competition. Biosafety Security LatAm is also a leader in security and automation of global solutions for governments and private sector facilities.
